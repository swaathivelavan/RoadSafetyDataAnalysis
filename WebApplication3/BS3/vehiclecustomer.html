Trend

The customers can access compare vehicle safely trends in order to make an informed decision before buying a vehicle. The customers input the vehicle type, brand, and other parameters to get the trend of the topmost safe vehicles over the specified range. For example, if a customer searches for the car by Toyota for the years 2000 to 2021, a graph is plotted which shows the accidents the vehicles have been a part of. The safest vehicle can be inferred from the graph by viewing the one that has the least number of accidents it has been involved in. (The line graph would have multiple lines for each car and hence provides the comparison in a visual format to the user). Also, the trend specifies if the safety features of the car have become better over the years and would give customers the confidence to buy it.
